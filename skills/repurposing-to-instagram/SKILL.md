# Repurposing LinkedIn to Instagram — Pato Bichara

Adapt approved LinkedIn posts for Instagram (@patobichara). Produces all deliverables needed for publication: adapted caption, format recommendation, and format-specific assets (Reel script or carousel breakdown).

## Before Adapting

1. Read the Instagram adaptation rules at [references/instagram-adaptation-rules.md](references/instagram-adaptation-rules.md)
2. For full strategy context, reference `/Users/patobichara/Code/pato-content-strategy/ESTRATEGIA-CONTENIDO.md` Section 4 (Instagram strategy)

## Workflow

### Step 1: Receive the LinkedIn Post

The user provides the full text of an approved LinkedIn post. If the user also provides context (which slot it was, which pillar, performance notes), use that to inform the adaptation.

### Step 2: Analyze and Recommend Format

Evaluate the LinkedIn post and recommend the best Instagram format:

**Recommend Reel when:**
- Single strong tip or insight deliverable in 30-60 seconds
- Topic lends itself to talking-head or visual storytelling
- Strong hook potential for the first 3 seconds of video
- The post is story-driven or has a clear "punchline"

**Recommend Carousel when:**
- Framework, list, or multi-point argument (3+ distinct points)
- Educational/how-to content
- The LinkedIn version was already a carousel or a long-form list post

**Recommend Static Post when:**
- Powerful quote or single reflection
- Short, punchy original
- Works as a designed image with text overlay (quote graphic)

Provide a 1-sentence rationale for the recommendation.

### Step 3: Adapt the Caption

Transform the LinkedIn text for Instagram:

1. **Shorten to 50-70%** of the original length
2. **Shift tone:** More conversational and direct. Less formal. Write as if speaking to a younger professional who admires Pato.
3. **Hook in first line:** This is the most critical line. Instagram's preview text cuts off — if the hook fails, the post is invisible.
4. **Restructure if needed:** LinkedIn's longer format may not translate directly. Reorganize around the single strongest point.
5. **Adapt the CTA:** LinkedIn CTAs (reflection questions) become Instagram CTAs:
   - "Guarda este post si te fue util"
   - "Tag a alguien que necesita leer esto"
   - "Comenta [emoji] si te identificas"
   - "Link en bio para mas"
6. **Remove LinkedIn-specific references:** No mention of "en este post de LinkedIn" or similar.
7. **Keep the LatAm lens.** Same perspective, adapted format.

## Guidelines

- **Nunca solo acortar — genuinamente adaptar.** Instagram no es LinkedIn resumido. El tono, la estructura, y el CTA cambian.
- **Hook > todo.** Instagram es mas competitivo que LinkedIn. Si la primera linea falla, el post no existe.
- **Saves son la metrica reina.** Contenido util que la gente guarda > contenido que solo recibe likes. Disenar para saves.
- **Mobile first.** Todo se ve en telefono. Texto grande, alto contraste, nada de parrafos densos.
- **Espanol por defecto.** Solo ingles si el post original estaba en ingles.
- **No pedir aclaraciones.** Tomar el post de LinkedIn y producir la adaptacion completa. Notar assumptions en "Notas para Beto."
